Delete Your Account

Last updated: 6 March 2026

We're sorry to see you go. You can delete your CartaraIQ account and all associated data by following the steps below.

How to Delete Your Account

1
Open CartaraIQ

Launch the CartaraIQ app on your device and sign in to your account.

2
Open Settings

Tap the Settings icon (gear) in the top-right corner of the main screen.

3
Go to Manage Data

Scroll down and tap "Manage My Data".

4
Delete Account

Tap "Delete My Account & Data". You will be asked to confirm. This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed from our servers:

  • Your account profile (name, email address)
  • All shopping lists and list items
  • Smart suggestion history and preferences
  • Push notification tokens
  • Biometric and PIN authentication data
⚠️ This action is permanent. Once your account is deleted, your data cannot be recovered. We recommend exporting your data before deletion by using the "Export My Data" option on the Manage Data screen.

Alternative: Contact Us

If you're unable to access the app or need assistance deleting your account, you can request deletion by emailing us at support@cartaraiq.app from the email address associated with your account. We will process your request within 48 hours.

Data Retention

After account deletion, all personal data is removed immediately. We do not retain any identifiable user data after deletion. Anonymous, aggregated analytics data that cannot be linked to your account may be retained.
